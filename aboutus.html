About Us

Seventy yeras bringing floral arrangments to the city of Limerick

Mill Flowers have been in existence since the 1950s. Three generations of our family have worked in this family business, artisticaly hand-arranging flowers for every occasion. We provide service from everyday bouques, birthday and holiday gift baskets, funeral baskets, special occasion arrangments, and more! No matter what the occasion, mill Flowers will put every ounce of love, dedication and personalisation to every piece that is created by us.
